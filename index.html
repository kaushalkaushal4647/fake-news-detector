📊 What we check

Capitalization patterns, sensational punctuation, harmful-health and conspiracy-style word pairs, clickbait phrases, and whether the language names real institutions or measured policy topics.

🎯 What you get

A REAL or FAKE label (based on score balance), a numeric confidence band, a misinformation risk tier, and a short list of concrete signals you can read and disagree with.

Educational tool. This page does not access live news databases or claim legal or journalistic authority. Treat output as a starting point for critical thinking, not a final judgment.
